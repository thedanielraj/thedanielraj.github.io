Student-ERP A local-first aviation training management system by Daniel Raj.

Built during my internship at Arunand's Aviation Institute, Bangalore. It unifies attendance tracking, fee recording, role-based access, receipt uploads, and Excel sync in one practical web app.

Problem Solved

From spreadsheet chaos to one operational dashboard

Student-ERP replaces scattered manual workflows with a structured interface for students, attendance, fees, and reporting.

Access Control

Role-based login

Superuser can manage everyone, while students view only their own attendance, fees, and remaining balance.

Attendance Engine

UI + Excel sync

Attendance is recorded in the web app and synchronized with attendance_master.xlsm for institute continuity.

Finance Workflow

Fee entries + receipts

Payment logging supports uploaded receipts and exposes student-facing balance tracking for transparency.
